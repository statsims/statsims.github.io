What this lab is showing

Noise: More vertical scatter usually weakens the correlation.

Outlier: One unusual point can change r a lot.

Curved pattern: A strong nonlinear pattern can still have a modest Pearson correlation because Pearson's r measures linear association.

Fitted model: The regression line can help visually summarize the linear trend, but the plot still matters more than the single number.
